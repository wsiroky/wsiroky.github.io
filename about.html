About Billy of Chili by Billy

Billy is a world-renowned chili chef. His chili took 2nd place at the Will's Northwood Inn Chili Cookoff in 2018, and won Honorable Mention and the People's Choice Award at the same competition in 2019. Coming into 2020, Billy has learned from the past and is the odds-on favorite to win the competition as well as a spot on the next season of Master Chef.

In addition to being possibly the greatest chili chef of all time, Billy is enrolled in the JD / MBA Program at Northwestern University. In his free time, he enjoys making chili, tasting chili, showing other people how to make chili, judging chili competitions, and drawing bowls of chili.
