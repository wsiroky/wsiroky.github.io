The Story

How Billy Became an Award-Winning Chili Chef

On a frigid day in January 2018, Billy happened to walk past a sign for a prestigious chili cookoff. Although Billy had never made chili before, he decided that it would be fun to enter anyway. At worst, he'd invite some friends, they'd try his chili, and they'd have a nice evening eating the chili all the professional chili chefs had made.

Billy entered the competition a few weeks later, and then something unexpected happen. The boy who had never made a bowl of chili in his life beat a litany of professional and semi-professional chili chefs to win second place. It was a true underdog story. Since then, Billy, and his chili, have become an international phenomenon.
